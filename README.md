# Rakuritu
**Rakuritu**, "Sunne-set", is a rhythm hack and slash game.

## Plot
You're Kagekiyo(īiÉ┤), an Ochimusha(fallen warrior) who just lost your liege in a dishonorable ambush; but not yet your head.
However the road home softens into shadow as the sun is about to set,
and the blurred figures waiting in the distance now resemble pursuers or bandits...

## Gameplay
In total ~4 minutes, hacking pursuers for a path and escaping before the dusky light fading.
For every second passed, the sun and dusk lower a bit.

## Building
Rakuritu is compiled with Open Watcom 2.0.
